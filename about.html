About

King of the Block turns walking into a quiet little turf war. Every street you walk gets painted green on your map. Add friends, and the same streets become territory: whoever has walked a street the most owns it — walk it more, and you take it back.

The app is local-first: your walks live on your device (backed up to your private iCloud), and the social layer is entirely optional. No ads, no tracking, no feed — just you, your streets, and the friends trying to steal them.

Walks can also come straight from Apple Health — runs, hikes and rides you’ve already recorded paint the map too. And if you try to claim streets from a train: we’ll notice. The conductor is the real King of the Block.
